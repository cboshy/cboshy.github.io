Designing

Animation Type: Selects which @keyframes (animation) to assign the DIV to

Duration: Determines the duration in seconds of the transition

Timing Function: Specifies the speed curve of the animation

Delay: Determines delay before animation starts in seconds

Iteration Count: Amount of times animation will play. Enter 10 for 'infinite'
